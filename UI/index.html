WORLD CLASS MAINTENANCE SERVICE

With M-T-A your maintenance requests can be tracked and gauranteed to be resolved immediately.



WHAT IS M-T-A

M-T-A (Maintenance - Tracker - App) is an online maintenance service assistant that allows you to create a maintenance request and track the progress of your request until it is resolved and completely done.

With M-T-A there are no worries of leaving your office to look for the maitenance guy or waiting for days to know the progress of your maitenance request, all you need to do is just track it right from your comfort.

HOW IT WORKS

CREATE A REQUEST

To create a new request you are required to fill in a request form. This form will contain details of your request (e.g Title, Description, and your Location/Department.)

AWAIT APPROVAL

Once your create a new request by submitting a request form, your are to wait for the maintenance department to read and approve your request. If your request is not approved it will be rejected.

TRACK PROGRESS

You can easily track the progress or your request and know how much work has been done by selecting the request or inputing the request id in the tracker.

GET NOTIFIED

As soon as your request as been fully completed and resolved, you will get a notification telling you the request has been completed and resolve.
